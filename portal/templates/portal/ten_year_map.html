Howe Dell Primary School, Hatfield, UK

July 2024

We completed the UK leg of our world tour with an enthusiastic reception from Year 4 students at Howe Dell Primary School in Hatfield. The pupils were captivated by our presentation on different types of robots and Ocado Group's automation technology used in sorting and packing deliveries. They were then tasked with designing robots to solve problems, producing creative concepts like robot childminders and homework helpers. Midway through, we introduced them to coding with Rapid Router, explaining algorithms and letting them experiment with the levels. The students were thrilled about the competition for the best robot design, which included a prize of a golden 3D printed medal. Teachers praised the engaging and educational workshop, while Ocado volunteers found the experience rewarding and beneficial for developing their communication and teaching skills. The children's designs were exceptional, making the judging process challenging, but ultimately a few standout innovations were selected.
