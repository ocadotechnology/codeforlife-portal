Code for Life will be launching a new game for secondary schools in 2018!

Following on from Rapid Router, this exciting game will build on students’ existing coding skills.

The game will be a time travelling adventure, which will take them to the next level on their coding journey. We’ll have a playable beta of Level 1 by early 2018, but first we’d like to get your thoughts on the gameplay, learning concept and general design.
